The Indian Air Force


Weapons

36 Dassault Rafale

The latest addition to India's aircraft arsenal; India has signed a deal for 36 Dassault Rafale multirole fighter aircraft. As of Oct 2021, 29 Rafale fighters are in service with the Indian Air Force.

Description

  • Name : Dassault Rafale
  • Type : Combat aircraft
  • Role: Multirole fighter
  • Origin Country: France

HAL Tejas

The HAL Tejas is an Indian multirole light fighter designed by the Aeronautical Development Agency (ADA) in collaboration with Aircraft Research and Design Centre (ARDC) of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) for the Indian Air Force and Indian Navy.

Description

  • Name : HAL Tejas
  • Type : Light combat aircraft
  • Role: Multirole light fighter
  • Origin Country: India

Helicopters

The HAL Dhruv is a utility helicopter designed and developed by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL). The development of HAL Dhruv was announced in November 1984.

Description

  • Name : HAL Dhruv
  • Type : Helicopter
  • Role: Utility helicopter
  • Origin Country: India

Trainer aircraft

The HAL HPT-32 Deepak is an Indian prop-driven primary trainer manufactured by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited. It has two seats in side-by-side configuration.

Description

  • Name : HAL HPT-32 Deepak
  • Type : Trainer aircraft
  • Role: Primary Trainer
  • Origin Country: India
